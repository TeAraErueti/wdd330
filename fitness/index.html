Purpose

We started planning back in 2023 when members of our team were collaborating with fitness professionals and experts in the field to bring forth "Fit For Life". Our platform launched late 2024 in hopes of assisting people in the challenges of modernised fitness planning. We are continually researching and adapting our site to our clients needs. As a group of fitness professionals, we have come together to share our knowledge and passion of fitness online to help you and many others. Our chosen platform of online services has allowed us to reach a broader audience and help more people than we could have ever imagined. We are here to help you with your fitness journey, whether you are a beginner or an expert. We look forward to working with you in your fitness planning and goals at Fit For Life.

Meet the Team

Meet Amy, Laurell, Jessie and Denise! So who are we? We are the team behind Fit For Life. We are a group of fitness professionals, mothers, and friends with varying backgrounds and experience in fitness. Amy is a qualified personal trainer and has been working in the fitness industry for over 10 years. Laurell is a national and international fitness competitor in body building, IFBB Pro physique, and bikini with over 7 years experience. Jessie is our marathon runner and HIIT trainer who loves to help people of all ages with their cardio, high intensity and strength workouts. Denise is a qualified yoga and pilates instructor and has been teaching for over 5 years. Together we have come up with the dream team, along with our connecting partners in the industry to deliver effective fitness plans for you. We look forward to working with you in helping you achieve your fitness goals here at Fit For Life.
